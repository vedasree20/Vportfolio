About Me

I’m Veda Sree, a second-year B.Tech student specializing in Information Technology at Sreenidhi Institute of Science & Technology. Academically driven with a CGPA of 9.51, I thrive in both academics and extracurriculars. My expertise in design spans nearly two years with NSS, along with an internship in social media design & as a part of Designing Team at the Student Developers Community. I've contributed to real-time projects like Project Bolt, a study platform for MBA students. With a strong skill set in Java, Python, frontend development, Figma, Canva, and Photoshop, I’m a fast learner with strengths in creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork.
